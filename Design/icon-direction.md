# App Icon Design Direction

This document details the visual concepts, materials, and layout guidelines for the ReMastera application icon on macOS.

## Core Design Concept: The Lightkeeper Lantern

The primary icon concept features a stylized frame-shaped lantern casting a warm amber glow. This directly references our mascot, Remy the Lightkeeper, and symbolizes bringing old footage into new light.

### Visual Composition

1. The Frame Base: A rounded macOS squircle representing a video frame. The frame has a metallic dark charcoal finish with subtle reflections along the top edge.
2. The Lantern Lens: The center of the squircle contains a translucent frosted glass lens, showing depth and refraction.
3. The Amber Filament: Inside the lens, a glowing warm amber filament is shaped like a classic film reel hub.
4. The Light Beam: A soft, progressive light cone projects forward from the lens, casting a warm gold gradient onto the bottom border.

## Alternative Concept: The Restored Frame

A split-frame squircle:

- Left Half: Matte textured charcoal finish with simulated noise grains.
- Right Half: Highly polished metallic finish with warm reflections, illustrating the transition from raw footage to a restored master.

## Visual Attributes

- Depth and Material: The icon must utilize native macOS Squircle geometry. It must show realistic lighting angles (shadows projecting from top-center to bottom).
- Gradients: Smooth transitions from charcoal to warm gold. Neon colors must be avoided.
- Monochrome Adaptation: The design must remain recognizable when scaled down to 16x16 pixels for menu bars or sidebars.
